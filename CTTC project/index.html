About BusKaro

Primary Online Bus Ticket Booking Platform in Odisha

Founded in 2017 in Bhubaneswar, India,BusKaro connects various cities across the state with just a click of a button.

BusKaro has revolutionized the online bus ticket booking industry by bringing together over 2,000 bus operators covering over many routes across the state and cities.

With its mobile app ,we will be getting our loyal customer base,BusKaro aims to be the largest community of bus travellers in the world in the late future.

  • BusKaro provides bus travellers, the most uncomplicated and hassle-free booking experience ever.
  • Choose your destination, select bus based on user ratings, view the seat layout, choose convenient seats, and book your ticket in just a few clicks!
  • A dedicated 24*7 customer-care team is available to cater to all the passengers needs while they are on-the- go.
  • With us, you will be able to book a bus ticket for Sleeper, AC, Semi- Luxury or Luxury coaches (as per your requirement) in a convenient way
  • We are the most reliable online portal, where you will be able to book bus tickets
